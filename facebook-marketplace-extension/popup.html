1. Open the vehicle tab Stay on the Bert Ogden inventory page for the car you want to post.
2. Click Quick Post The helper saves the draft and opens Facebook Marketplace for you.
3. Let it finish It will try to complete step one, move forward, and optionally click Publish automatically.

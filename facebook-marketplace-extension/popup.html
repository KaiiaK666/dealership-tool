Quick post vehicle

While you're on a Bert Ogden vehicle page, click once. The helper builds the draft, opens Facebook Marketplace, and tries to fill the form automatically.

1. Open the vehicle tab Stay on the Bert Ogden inventory page for the car you want to post.
2. Click Quick Post The helper saves the draft and opens Facebook Marketplace for you.
3. Review and publish Double-check Facebook's form, add images if needed, and finish the post.

Bert Ogden Dealership Alerts

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bert Ogden Dealership Alerts collects, uses, and protects information connected to our dealership text messaging program and related operational notifications.

What information we collect

We may collect and store the following information when a user is enrolled in alerts:

  • Name, role, dealership affiliation, and contact details.
  • Mobile phone number and messaging preferences.
  • Message delivery records, response records, and support requests.
  • Operational details needed to send assignment, appointment, and workflow alerts.

How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Send transactional dealership alerts and operational SMS notifications.
  • Coordinate lead assignments, appointment follow-up, and internal workflow updates.
  • Maintain service quality, message delivery, security, and audit records.
  • Respond to support requests and manage opt-in or opt-out preferences.

SMS program details

Bert Ogden Dealership Alerts sends transactional text messages related to dealership staff notifications, lead assignments, appointment coordination, and other operational updates. Message frequency varies based on activity and user settings. Message and data rates may apply.

Mobile privacy commitment:

Mobile information, including text messaging originator opt-in data and consent, will not be shared, sold, rented, or transferred to third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Sharing of information

We may share limited information only with service providers that help us deliver or support the messaging program, maintain our systems, or satisfy legal obligations. Those providers may access information only as needed to perform services on our behalf and are not authorized to use it for their own marketing purposes.

Data retention and security

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for operations, compliance, support, and recordkeeping. We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Your choices

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at (956) 429-8898.

Related terms

Additional program information is available in our Terms and Conditions.
